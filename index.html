About us

ALBA S.r.l. is a company specialized in renovations, restorations and technical installations. We operate throughout Italy with an approach oriented towards quality, punctuality and customer satisfaction. We support architects, companies, public and private entities in customized projects, offering a complete service: from technical consultancy to final delivery.

For us, every intervention is an opportunity to build value, with attention to detail and respect for every environment.

Serving All of Lombardia

Based in Milan, Alba S.r.l delivers renovation excellence across the country. Our network of trusted professionals allows us to serve clients in every region with the same dedication and expertise.

Façade and Exterior Renovation

At ALBA S.r.l., we specialize in exterior construction and renovation that not only enhances the beauty of your building—but ensures its strength and safety.

Seismic Reinforcement Work

Structural upgrades designed to protect buildings from earthquake damage, using advanced anti-seismic techniques.

Thermal and Acoustic Insulation

Exterior insulation systems (cappotto termico) that improve energy efficiency and interior comfort.

Plastering and Façade Painting

High-quality exterior finishing, restoring buildings with long-lasting, weather-resistant coatings.

We deliver durable, regulation-compliant solutions tailored to your building’s specific needs— whether residential, commercial, or public.

Interior Renovation and Fit-Outs

From initial concept to final finish, Alba S.r.l transforms interior spaces with meticulous attention to detail. Our services include:

  • Flooring installation (tiles, wood, resin)

We help businesses and property owners reimagine their interiors into modern, functional, and welcoming spaces.
