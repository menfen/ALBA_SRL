About Us

External Interventions and Facade Redevelopment

ALBA S.r.l. specializes in enhancing exteriors, with interventions that combine aesthetics, durability and compliance with regulations.

Our services include:

Restoration and renovation of facades

Thermal coat and external insulation

Waterproofing and roofing work

Painting and external finishes

Construction of sidewalks, fences and outdoor spaces

We take care of every phase: from technical survey to implementation, with certified materials and tailor-made solutions.

We work for condominiums, companies, public and private entities that wish to renovate and protect their property over time.

Restorations and Interior Works

ALBA S.r.l. deals with the recovery and transformation of interior spaces, combining construction tradition and contemporary solutions.

From conservative restoration to complete renovation, we intervene with artisanal care and technical vision.

Our services include:

Restoration of historic interiors and period apartments

Turnkey renovations for residential and commercial properties

Construction and installation of flooring (ceramic, parquet, resin)

Plasterboard, false ceilings, acoustic and thermal finishes

Painting, lighting and coordinated design of the rooms

We work alongside private individuals, companies and professionals in the sector, guaranteeing tailor-made results, quality materials and respect for deadlines.

Restoring means enhancing. Renovating means transforming.With ALBA S.r.l., every space finds its identity again.
