Façade and Exterior Renovation

Alba S.r.l offers comprehensive exterior renovation services for commercial and residential buildings. We specialize in:

  • Façade restoration and painting
  • Thermal insulation systems (cappotto termico)
  • Waterproofing and roof maintenance
  • Concrete repair and structural recovery

Our team ensures that every exterior project enhances the building’s visual appeal while increasing its energy efficiency and compliance with local building standards.

Interior Renovation and Fit-Outs

From initial concept to final finish, Alba S.r.l transforms interior spaces with meticulous attention to detail. Our services include:

We help businesses and property owners reimagine their interiors into modern, functional, and welcoming spaces.
